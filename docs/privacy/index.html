Privacy Policy

Effective: March 22, 2025

Overview

PourSort is a color sorting puzzle game by The Knack. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data We Collect

None. PourSort stores game progress (level, scores, settings) locally on your device only. No data leaves your device. No analytics, no tracking, no advertising SDKs.

In-App Purchases

PourSort offers an optional one-time Pro unlock managed through Apple's App Store. All billing is handled by Apple. We do not access your payment information.

No Advertising

PourSort does not display ads. We do not use any advertising networks or share data with advertisers.

Children's Privacy

PourSort does not collect any data from anyone, including children.
