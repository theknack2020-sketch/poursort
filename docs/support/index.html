How to Play

Tap a tube to pick up the top ball, then tap another tube to place it. You can only place a ball on the same color or in an empty tube. Fill each tube with a single color to complete the level.

Undo & Restart

Use the Undo button to take back your last move. Restart resets the level to its original state. Pro users get unlimited undo.

Restore Purchases

If you've purchased Pro and need to restore it, go to the Pro screen and tap "Restore Purchases".

Daily Challenge

A new puzzle every day! All players get the same puzzle. Complete it to build your daily streak.
